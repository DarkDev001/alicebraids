Alice Titilayo

Greetings, my name is Alice Titilayo '@alicebraidss'. My passion for braiding was inspired by my mother, who was a hairstylist in Nigeria. As a little girl, I would find anything that had strands that I could braid, even if it wasn't hair. The passion grew solemnly with me. It was my mother’s dream for her to braid internationally. As a college student, I braided to support my parents. My journey to America still didn’t stop me from chasing my dreams, even though there are so many other things I'm sure I could do. As a way of expanding my knowledge, I took the famous “stitch braid” class, taught by Nasthestylist, and bloomed like never before. I'm still opened to improve and learning more, because for me, this is more than just a passion, it is my legacy. I am 43 years old.

PLEASE READ BEFORE BOOKING

. Please be on time for your appointment. Please do not ring the doorbell. When you arrive, please call the number available in your confirmation.

. Alice is only available for braids and locks.

. A $30 deposit is required when booking (an increased deposit on selected premium styles). All deposits go towards the final cost of your style. A processing fee may be added at checkout.

. Final payments are paid through Cash, Apple Pay or Cash App.

. Appointments are available 70 days in advance.

. Clients must be 10 years and older to book.

. Guests / children are not allowed at the appointment.

. Braiding hair is provided for all services, except for human hair extensions and the curly hair for the S-Part Large Knotless. Please send a text 24 hours before your appointment to confirm if your required color is in stock. Ruwa Hair (click for photo) is also available.

. Contact information for Bora Braids is sent electronically after booking online.

. Please notify Alice at least 48 hours before your scheduled appointment to cancel.

. Same day style change is PROHIBITED.

. Deposits are non-refundable unless I have to reschedule or cancel (also non-transferable).

LAST MINUTE APPOINTMENTS:

. Last minute and VIP Appointment requests are permitted. An additional charge of $100 is required. Please visit borabraids.com/vip to book.

LATE POLICY:

. I allow a 10-minutes grace period without a late fee. At 11-15 minutes = $20 late fee (if time permits) or cancelled. 20 minutes past = appointment is cancelled.

. No Call No Show = Cancelled (DEPOSIT IS AUTOMATICALLY INVALID WITH 50% OF THE SERVICE COST BEING CHARGED.)

. Deposits are non-refundable unless I have to reschedule or cancel (also non-transferable).

. No major style changes on the day of appointment.

. Bookings are available 70 days in advance.

HAIR REQUIREMENTS:

. Hair must be cleaned and product free (no more than 3 days).

. Hair must be completely detangled and blow dried from roots to tips for all styles (no exceptions). Click here for visual representation.

. Lack of preparation will result in your appointment being cancelled and deposit forfeited or a fee.

. Hair must be at least 3 inches all over.

. If you have any scalp conditions; bald spots, thin edges, hair breakages, please inform me.

. Please be patient, hair styles may take longer on different clients' based on hair texture.

. Click here for hair length chart.
